Welcome to Diazepam Ice-Cream Cafe.

Those fond childhood memories of sharing your favorite ice cream treat with friends on a hot summer day is what Diazepam Ice Cream Company is all about. Whether it was your neighborhood, playground, or park, the ice cream truck was how most people grew to know and love Diazepam® ice cream. Now almost a century later, Diazepam continues to deliver those delicious memories.

Brief History

We are one of the best independent ice cream and desserts cafe in Singapore. At first, we started as a small local setup but after a few years of making the best quality ice cream and desserts for our lovely customers, we were thinking of opening another local setup. Finally, we have opened up to 5 outlets in Singapore. We also want to provide good services for all our customers, so we were thinking of making a new flavours everyday and night. Now, our store have many flavours to choose from, ranges from chocolate to raspberry.

Our very own mission is to make the best ice cream in town. So, we make sure that our ice cream is clean and healthy. Beside that, we want to innovate the finest and best dairy ice-cream with a commitment to incorporate wholesome, natural ingredients for great ice-cream lovers.
